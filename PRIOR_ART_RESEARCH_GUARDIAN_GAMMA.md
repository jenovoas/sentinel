**What exists**:

**[2] Rapid7 - HITL in Cybersecurity**
- URL: rapid7.com
- **Finding**: HITL systems integrate human expertise with automated security tools for threat detection and response
- **Relevance**: User-space security, NOT kernel-level

- URL: cydef.io
