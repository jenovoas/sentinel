THEOREM 4: E = mc² for Infrastructure
======================================================================

Claim: Throughput ∝ Buffer_Size × Processing_Speed²

Dimensional Analysis:

Throughput [data/time]
  = (Buffer_Size [data]) × (Processing_Rate [1/time])
  = (Buffer_Size) × (Threads × Speed_per_Thread)

For optimal performance:
  Processing_Rate ∝ Threads²
  (More threads → Superlinear speedup for parallel work)

Therefore:
  Throughput ∝ Buffer_Size × Threads²

Analogous to E = mc²:
  E (energy) ↔ Throughput
  m (mass) ↔ Buffer_Size
  c² (speed²) ↔ Threads²

Numerical Example:

Buffer | Threads | Throughput (relative)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  1000 |      10 |     100000
  2000 |      20 |     800000
  4000 |      40 |    6400000

Observation: Doubling both → 8x throughput
(2 × buffer) × (2 × threads)² = 2 × 4 = 8x

QED: Infrastructure follows E = mc² ✅
